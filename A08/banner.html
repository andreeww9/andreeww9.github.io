📜 Pre-Release and Development (2017–2020)

●2017: Development of Genshin Impact began, reportedly with a team of about 120 people. It was heavily inspired by The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, though it evolved its own identity over time.

●June 2019: Genshin Impact was officially announced at E3 and ChinaJoy. The game drew early attention for its similarities to Breath of the Wild, leading to mixed reactions — especially from the gaming community in China.

●2019–2020: Closed beta tests were conducted globally to gather feedback and optimize gameplay mechanics, story, and UI.

🚀 Launch and Initial Success (2020)

●September 28, 2020: Genshin Impact officially launched on Windows, PlayStation 4, Android, and iOS. It was a free-to-play game with gacha mechanics to obtain characters and weapons.

●The game became an instant hit, praised for:

*High production values.

*Expansive open world.

*Engaging elemental combat system.

*Frequent content updates.

●Within two weeks of launch, it reportedly earned $100 million, recouping its development costs.
